Welcome To Our BHSS Family

Dedicated to Excellence

Welcome to Borbali Higher Secondary School, where education meets excellence! At BHHS, we believe in nurturing the minds of our students to prepare them for the challenges and opportunities of tomorrow. With a dedicated team of passionate educators and state-of-the-art facilities, we provide a dynamic learning environment that fosters academic achievement, personal growth, and the development of essential life skills. Our curriculum is designed to inspire curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity in our students. Whether they're exploring the realms of mathematics, delving into the intricacies of science, expressing themselves through the arts, or engaging in physical activities, we strive to instill a love for learning that lasts a lifetime. Beyond academics, we prioritize the holistic development of each student. Through a range of extracurricular activities, community service initiatives, and leadership opportunities, we encourage our students to explore their passions, discover their strengths, and become well-rounded individuals who make positive contributions to society..

What do we AIM for?

Our mission vision & objectives

We work hard to improve student ACHIEVEMENT

We are ready for student's doubts and highly encourage them to express their doubts to us. For extra help for the students we have "Whatsapp Group" for each subjects.

Preparing students for successful FUTURE

From an initial stage we prepare our students to grasp higher quality education. We encourage students to take "Advance mathematics" so that they could get an idea of class 11th and 12th mathematics..

Providing healthy learning ENVIRONMENT

A healthy learning environment is the key to get the attention of the students. We in BHSS provides ideal environment for students to study which includes multiple fans in single class, clean drinking water, toilet and much more.
